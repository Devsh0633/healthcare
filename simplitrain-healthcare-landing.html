The problem today

Your training team is doing the job of three systems

A scheduling tool, a course library, and a spreadsheet for everything the other two don't cover. SimpliTrain replaces all three.

Training scheduling built for clinical sessions

Skills labs need a room, a manikin, and a certified instructor at the same time. SimpliTrain's scheduling engine books all three together for ILT and VILT sessions alike, so a double-booked skills lab stops being a monthly event.
